01 — Getting Started

Welcome to TaskForge Pro, the world's most advanced micro-task ecosystem. Our platform connects global users with advertisers and sponsors who pay for real human engagement — surveys, video views, app installations, social actions, and more. After registration, your account is instantly active.

02 — Task Marketplace

The Task Marketplace hosts up to 50 verified tasks refreshing every 24 hours. Each tile shows the category, reward amount, estimated time, and difficulty. To prevent fraud, every task requires a unique Verification Token issued by the advertiser upon completion. Click Verify, complete the task, return with your token.

03 — Daily Faucet

Claim small rewards every 5 minutes, up to 288 times daily. Base reward is $0.0025 per claim. Boost your rate by maintaining streaks, achieving higher tier membership, or building referrals. Every claim requires Cloudflare Turnstile human verification.

04 — Consistency Tracker

The 10-Day Professional Consistency Tracker is our flagship gamification engine. Complete your daily quota of 50 tasks for 10 consecutive days to unlock the $30 Premium Bonus Tier. Missing a single day resets the tracker to Day 1 — plan accordingly and maintain your streak.

05 — Wallet & Withdrawals

The Secure Wallet consolidates all earnings. Withdraw via instant Crypto Transfer (BTC, ETH, USDT, LTC) with a 15–20% processing fee and $1.00 minimum. Or redeem Digital Gift Cards for Amazon, Google Play, Flipkart, Paytm and more — zero fees, instant 16-digit redeem codes. India users receive automatic INR conversion at live rates with denomination options (₹100, ₹500, ₹1000).

06 — Referral Program

Share your unique link and earn 15% commission on all referred user earnings permanently, with no cap. Tier 2 sub-referrals earn you 5%. Find your referral link in the Referral Hub section.

07 — Anti-Fraud Policy

TaskForge Pro employs advanced fraud detection including IP analysis, device fingerprinting, behavioral analytics, and token verification. VPN use, bots, multiple accounts, or any fraudulent activity results in permanent ban and balance forfeiture without appeal.
